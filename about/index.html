My days are spent grinding online MTTs across GGPoker Ontario, 888poker, and ACR, often with up to 12 tables running at once.

On stream and across my content, you won't find highlight-reel-only poker. You'll get real sessions, real strategy breakdowns, and real swings — the deep runs and the brutal bubbles alike. No pretending variance doesn't exist. No selling a dream. Just what it actually takes to play this game for a living.
